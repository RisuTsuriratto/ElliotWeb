The main essence of Elliot

Elliot is a colourful 2D platformer inspired in 16-bit classics yet with a hardcore touch.

Run, jump and dash through over 60+ levels to solve the mystery surrounding the World of Elliot, Manuel and the adorable Mombas.

It’s cute but…

Don’t be misled by appearances: it’s a colorful game, Elliot and Manuel are so cute, but it’s a tough game.

Really it’s technicolor hell!
